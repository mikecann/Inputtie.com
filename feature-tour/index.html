Feature Tour

  • One Mouse & Keyboard

  • Have you ever wished there was a simple way you could use the mouse and keyboard from your PC on your laptop at the same time without messing about unplugging USB cables or buying expensive switching equipment?

    Well, look no further, Inputtie is here! Control all of your devices with the mouse and keyboard of one computer. No need to leave your seat, just slide your mouse cursor from your main computer screen to your laptop screen. With one movement of the mouse you can switch control between any active device.

  • Simple To Use

  • Installation and setup takes only 2 minutes!

    Inputtie detects all of your computers automatically. All you have to do is select which computer to connect.

    Our user interface is logical and intuitive. Simply, drag arrows to create connections between available devices. Inputtie will remember all your device connections so you can immediately reconnect next time you boot up.

  • Share Clipboard

  • Copy and paste has never been so powerful!

    Write a document on multiple machines. Copy text from your desktop computer into an email on your laptop. Inputtie helps you multi-task faster than ever before.

  • Multi-platform

  • Inputtie is available for Windows, Apple and Linux operating systems.

    Connecting your multi-platform devices together is quick and easy.

    Don't lose time due to an innefficient workspace. Inputtie's multi-platform device management will save you hours!
